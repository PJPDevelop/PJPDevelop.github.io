Permissions

Microphone and Screen Recording

From version 2.1 and up, the app supports exporting the EAS Alerts as videos. In order to do that, it performs screen recording to capture what's on screen and uses the microphone to capture the audio produced by the alert.

Screen and audio recording stop when you finish playing/recording the EAS Alert.

Any recorded data is simply used to generate a video file, which you can use and share at your own will, and none of this data leaves your device nor does PJP Develop have access to it.

You can revoke these permissions at will if you do not wish to export any EAS Alerts as videos.

Access to Device's File Storage (Read/Write)

From version 2.1 and up, the app supports impoting/exporting the EAS Alerts files, so that they can be backed up or shared with friends.

You can revoke these permissions if you do not wish to import/export EAS Alerts to files.
